🎯 Gesture Control Hub

Control your PC with hand gestures — no hardware required

🖐️

Air Mouse

Control your mouse cursor and click using hand gestures in mid-air.

  • Index finger → Move cursor
  • Thumb + Index pinch → Left click
  • Two-finger pinch → Right click
  • Open palm swipe → Scroll
LAUNCH
🎨

Air Canvas

Draw in the air — your canvas is the camera feed.

  • Index finger → Draw
  • All fingers up → Pause
  • Fist → Clear canvas
  • Peace / Thumbs / Pinky → Change color
LAUNCH
🎵

Media Controller

Play, pause, skip tracks and control volume hands-free.

  • Open palm → Play / Pause
  • Swipe left/right → Prev / Next track
  • Two fingers up/down → Volume
  • Fist → Mute / Unmute
LAUNCH
📊

Presentation Controller

Navigate your slides without touching the keyboard.

  • Swipe right → Next slide
  • Swipe left → Previous slide
  • Thumbs up → Start slideshow (F5)
  • Fist → End slideshow (Esc)
LAUNCH
🤟

Sign Language

Real-time ASL alphabet detection — spell out words with your hands.

  • Supports A, B, C, D, E, F, I, L, O, V, W, Y
  • Hold sign ~1 second to register letter
  • Space & Clear buttons for word building
  • Confidence percentage shown live
